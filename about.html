    • About Me

    I am an artist currently attending Stony Brook University in New York. I have been studying and practicing art with intentions of working with it professionally since 2008, when I set my sights on the gaming and entertainment industries.

    I believe that the advancing technologies that these industries use present me with an opportunity to create entirely new worlds and stories for viewers to see and explore. Not trying to be a part of the creation of such things would be denying some of my earliest childhood dreams.

    I prefer to work mainly with digital mediums, using programs such as Blender 3d and Photoshop to create my projects. In addition to those two programs, I also work in Zbrush, Unity, After Effects, and Illustrator.
